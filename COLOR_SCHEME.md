# Pegasus Flight - Color Scheme

## Navy Blue & White Theme with Warm Accents

### Primary Colors

**Navy Blue** - Main brand color
- Primary: `#003366` (Navy Blue)
- Primary Light: `#0055A5` (Lighter Navy)
- Primary Dark: `#001F3F` (Darker Navy)

**White** - Clean, readable backgrounds
- Used for card backgrounds, text on navy
- Ensures high contrast for outdoor visibility

### Accent Colors

**Warm Orange** - Secondary accent for CTAs and highlights
- Secondary: `#FF9933` (Warm Orange)
- Secondary Light: `#FFB366` (Light Orange)
- Used for: Action buttons, important highlights

**Gold** - Tertiary accent
- Tertiary: `#FFD700` (Gold)
- Used for: Special highlights, premium features

**Green** - Success states
- Success: `#28A745` (Green)
- Used for: Completed checklist items, checkmarks

### Neutral Grays

Light to dark grays for UI elements:
- Gray100: `#E1E1E1` - Very light backgrounds
- Gray200: `#C8C8C8` - Light borders
- Gray300: `#ACACAC` - Subtle borders
- Gray400: `#919191` - Inactive elements
- Gray500: `#6E6E6E` - Secondary text (completed items)
- Gray600: `#404040` - Body text
- Gray900: `#212121` - Dark text
- Gray950: `#141414` - Nearly black

## Usage Examples

### Home Screen
- Background: Navy Blue (#003366)
- Title Text: White
- Button Cards: White background with navy text
- Icons: Navy Blue

### Checklists
- Header: Navy Blue background, white text
- Progress Bar: White on navy
- List Background: Light gray (#F5F5F5)
- Card Background: White
- Unchecked Border: Gray300 (#ACACAC)
- Checked Border: Success Green (#28A745)
- Checkbox (unchecked): Gray400 border
- Checkbox (checked): Green background with white checkmark
- Text (unchecked): Black
- Text (checked): Gray500 with strikethrough

### Buttons
- Primary Action: Warm Orange (#FF9933) - "Reset Checklist"
- Navigation: Navy Blue (#003366)
- Destructive: Keep system red

## Accessibility

✅ **High Contrast**: Navy blue on white exceeds WCAG AAA standards
✅ **Outdoor Readable**: Navy and white provide maximum readability in sunlight
✅ **Color Blind Friendly**: Uses distinct values, not just hue differences
✅ **Large Touch Targets**: 75px+ minimum height for all interactive elements

## Design Philosophy

This color scheme is optimized for:
1. **Professionalism** - Navy conveys trust and aviation heritage
2. **Visibility** - High contrast for outdoor/cockpit use
3. **Safety** - Clear visual hierarchy with warm accents for actions
4. **Simplicity** - Clean, uncluttered interface focused on usability
